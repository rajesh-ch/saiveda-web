Computer Networking

A home network will allow you to connect multiple computers to a single internet connection. Giving you complete flexibility to use your computer where you want, when you want. With a network in place you’ll be able to connect multiple computers with shared printers, hard drives and to the internet, meaning your files, music and pictures are only a few clicks away no matter what computer you’re on, room you’re in or desk you’re sat at.

  • Wireless or Cabled?
  • Connect & Share!
  • Safe & Secure

We can install both wired and wireless networks. Although you can connect most items using a wired network, more people are now choosing wireless because of the extra flexibility. It’s easier to move items or connect mobile devices to a wireless network. Using security software and encryption technologies, you control which computers or users can access what items on your network making it totally secure. When we install your network, we’ll ensure there is sufficient security installed to protect your equipment and data.
